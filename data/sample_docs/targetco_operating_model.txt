TargetCo — Operating Model & Organizational Summary
Company: TargetCo Solutions LLC
Headquarters: Austin, TX
Employees: 320
Annual Revenue: $38M

Organization:
TargetCo uses a product-line organizational structure:
- Product Line A (GM: Kevin O'Brien, 95 employees) — enterprise analytics
- Product Line B (GM: Priya Sharma, 85 employees) — SMB dashboards
- Shared Services (Director: Tom Nguyen, 60 employees) — IT, HR, Finance
- Sales & Partnerships (VP: Rachel Adams, 55 employees)
- Customer Support (Director: Mike Johnson, 25 employees)

Note: Finance is handled by a 6-person team under Shared Services. No dedicated CFO.
Note: Engineering is split across product lines with no centralized CTO function.

Key Systems:
- CRM: HubSpot Professional
- Accounting: QuickBooks Enterprise
- HRIS: BambooHR
- Project Management: Asana
- Communication: Microsoft Teams, Google Meet
- Data: PostgreSQL (self-managed), Google BigQuery
- BI: Looker
- Marketing: HubSpot (built-in)

Key Processes:
- Lead-to-Cash: HubSpot → QuickBooks, manual invoice generation, 60-day avg sales cycle
- Hiring: BambooHR, informal onboarding (no documented program)
- Financial Close: Monthly close by Day 12, manual reconciliation in spreadsheets
- Customer Onboarding: 30-day average, tracked in Asana

KPIs:
- Gross Margin: 62%
- Customer Retention Rate: 85%
- NPS: 38
- Employee Retention: 82%
- ARR Growth: 15% YoY

Vendors & Contracts:
- Google Cloud Platform ($600K/year, month-to-month)
- HubSpot Professional ($120K/year)
- Asana Business ($35K/year)
- BambooHR ($28K/year)

Geographic Presence:
- United States only (Austin HQ, remote employees across 12 states)
- No international presence

Known Issues:
- Engineering teams have inconsistent coding standards across product lines
- Customer support uses a shared Gmail inbox rather than a ticketing system
- No documented disaster recovery or business continuity plan
- Several key processes rely on tribal knowledge held by 2-3 individuals

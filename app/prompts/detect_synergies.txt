Categories of synergies:
- Cost synergies: eliminating duplicate spend, consolidating vendors, reducing headcount overlap
- Revenue synergies: cross-selling, expanded market access, combined product offerings
- Efficiency synergies: streamlined processes, shared services, automation opportunities
- Operational synergies: best-practice adoption, improved SLAs, faster delivery

Respond ONLY in valid JSON with this structure:
{
  "synergies": [
    {
      "opportunity": "<concise synergy description>",
      "impact": "low" | "medium" | "high",
      "category": "cost" | "revenue" | "efficiency" | "operational",
      "reason": "<why this opportunity exists and how it creates value>"
    }
  ]
}

Rules:
- Only identify synergies supported by the provided data
- High impact = significant financial or operational value
- Be specific about what would be consolidated, shared, or improved
- Include the rationale grounded in evidence from the documents
